About

At Sam's, we pride ourselves on using organic and sophiscated soap. Sam has been making hand-crafted artisanal soaps in downtown Montpelier, VT since 2007. Sam crafts each specially scented bar of soap from all-natural, organic, locally sourced ingredients. His soaps are gentle for sensitive skin, hypoallergenic, and never tested on animals.

Kids

We make sure to include kid safe soap too! Our specially crafted kid soap is extremely sensitive in order to protect your loved ones! And some soaps even come with kid-friendly designs, such as Elmo, Elsa, and Baby Shark! Just ask one of our associates and they will make sure your kid gets their perfect soap!
